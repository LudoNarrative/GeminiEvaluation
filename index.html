These are the sorts of rules you might find in these games:

  • The resource bar can go down or up
  • Red and blue triangles can appear or disappear
  • Blue circles can appear or disappear in the background
  • Triangles can change the direction they are moving
  • Any of the above might happen:
    • ...when triangles of the same or different colors touch
    • ...every now and then
    • ...while the mouse is pressed
    • ...when the mouse clicks a triangle
  • Also, triangles might move in these ways, and sometimes only when the mouse is held:
    • By clicking and dragging
    • Towards/away from the cursor
    • In a circle around the cursor
    • Towards/away from other triangles
    • Going forwards
